## Typography
- **Font Family:** Montserrat (Google Fonts)
  - Montserrat-Regular (400) — body text
  - Montserrat-Bold (700) — headings, CTAs
  - Montserrat-SemiBold (600) — subheadings, labels
- **Body size:** ~16px (1rem)
- **Heading size:** 32px (h1), scaling down
- **Line height:** 1.5 (body), 1.2 (headings)

## Visual Style
- **Overall feel:** Premium, clean, trust-building. Science/tech meets comfort.
- **Whitespace:** Generous. Airy layouts with breathing room.
- **Corners:** Rounded (8-12px on cards, 24px+ on CTA buttons)
- **Shadows:** Minimal — subtle elevation on cards, none on most elements
- **Category icons:** Circular thumbnails on light blue (#C1ECFB) backgrounds
- **Nav style:** Clean horizontal, Montserrat, navy text, white background
- **CTA buttons:** Navy (#1A2B58) bg, white text, rounded-full, bold
- **No gradients** on primary UI — flat, solid colors
- **Product cards:** White bg, clean borders, generous padding

## Brand Tone for Internal Tools
For an internal tool, the brand should be:
- Navy sidebar (matches brand navy #1A2B58)
- White content area
- Blue accent (#3B9AD2) for interactive elements, links, active states
- Light blue (#E8F4F8) for highlighted sections, hover states
- Montserrat font throughout
- Rounded corners (8px cards, 6px inputs, rounded-full for pills/badges)
- Clean, generous spacing — not cramped
